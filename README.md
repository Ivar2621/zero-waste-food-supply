# zero-waste-food-supply
# 🌱 Zero Waste Smart Food Supply Chain

An AI-powered platform to reduce food waste by connecting surplus food sources with NGOs and communities in need.

---

## 🚀 Overview

**Zero Waste Smart Food Supply Chain** is designed to solve one of the biggest global problems — food wastage.

The system uses AI-driven logic to:
- Predict food expiry
- Forecast demand
- Suggest smart pricing
- Connect suppliers with NGOs
- Optimize redistribution using maps

---

## 🎯 Problem

Restaurants, supermarkets, and warehouses waste large amounts of food daily, while many people still suffer from food shortages.

---

## 💡 Solution

This platform bridges the gap by:
- Identifying surplus food
- Matching it with nearby NGOs
- Recommending best actions (sell, discount, donate)
- Providing real-time map-based insights

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Food Analysis** – Predict expiry & demand
- 💰 **Smart Pricing** – Dynamic discount suggestions
- 🗺️ **Live Map** – View NGOs & suppliers nearby
- 🔄 **Smart Matching** – AI-based redistribution routes
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard** – Impact tracking
- 🔐 **Login System** – User authentication (frontend)

---

**Frontend:**
